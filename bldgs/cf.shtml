Communications Facility (CF)

The Communications Facility is an Academic building (map) (building photo tour) (Google directions).

ACCESSIBILITY

  • Accessible entrances:
    • Button activated entrances are located on the east and west sides of the building
  • Accessibility notes:
    • There are accessible restrooms located on all floors
    • Centrally located elevators provide access to all floors
  • Accessible parking is available to the east (Lot 17 G)
  • See also Campus Accessibility Information
