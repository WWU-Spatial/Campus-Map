Environmental Studies (ES)

The Environmental Studies building is an Academic building (map) (building photo tour) (Google directions)

ACCESSIBILITY

  • Accessible entrances:
    • Button activated entrances are located on the north and south sides of the building
  • Accessibility notes:
    • The west elevator provides access to all floors including split levels
    • The east elevator only provides access to whole levels
    • Accessible restrooms are located on the ground floor
    • Access to Arntzen Hall is provided on the ground floor
  • Accessible parking to the east (Lot 17-G)
  • See also Campus Accessibility Information
