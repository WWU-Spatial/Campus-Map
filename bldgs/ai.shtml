Academic Instructional Center East (AI)

The Academic Instructional Center is an Academic building (map) (building photo tour) (Google directions). See also the Academic Instructional Center West building.

ACCESSIBILITY

  • Accessible parking to the east and west of the building
  • Accessible entrances
    • Button activated entrances are located on the north and east sides of the building
  • Accessibility notes:
    • Accessible restrooms are located on each floor
    • Both elevators offer access to all floors
    • Academic Instructional Center West is accessible via the 4th floor sky-bridge
  • See also Campus Accessibility Information.
