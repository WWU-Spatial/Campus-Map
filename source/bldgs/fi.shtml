Fine Arts Building (FI)

The Fine Arts building is an Academic building (map) (Google directions).

ACCESSIBILITY

  • Accessible entrances:
    • A button activated entrance is located on the northwest side of the building
    • An accessible manual entrance is located on the southwest side of the building
  • Accessibility notes:
    • An elevator provides access to the first and second floors
    • The third floor and basement are not wheelchair accessible
    • Rooms 123, 125 and 125a are accessible via wheelchair lift
    • Accessible restrooms are located on the first floor
    • Computer lab on 1st floor at north end is accessible
    • Western Gallery: northwest entrance is accessible
  • Accessible parking to the southeast
  • See also Campus Accessibility Information
