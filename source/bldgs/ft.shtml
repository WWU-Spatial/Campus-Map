Fairhaven Towers ("Stacks" # 1 - 12)

Fairhaven Towers are University Residence buildings (map) (University Residences Tour) (Google directions). See also the Fairhaven College Academic building

ACCESSIBILITY

  • Accessible entrance:
    • Fairhaven Towers 1-10 are not accessible
    • A button activated entrance is located on the east side of the building in the courtyard for Fairhaven Towers 11 & 12
  • Accessibility notes:
    • Wheelchair access is through main courtyard entrance
    • Stacks 11 & 12 are accessible
    • An elevator provides access to all floors of Fairhaven Towers 11 & 12
    • An accessible restroom is located in the stack 11 fourth floor TV lounge
  • Accessible parking to the west
  • See also Campus Accessibility Information
