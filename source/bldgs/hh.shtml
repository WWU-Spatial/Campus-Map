Haggard Hall (HH)

Haggard Hall is a Library and Campus Services building (map) (building photo tour) (Google directions). See also Wilson Library (connected via a sky bridge on the second level).

ACCESSIBILITY

  • Accessible entrances:
    • Button activated entrances are located on the northwest, northeast, and southeast sides of the building
    • The northeast entrance is located under the Haggard Hall/Wilson Library sky-bridge
  • Accessibility notes:
    • Elevators provide access to all floors
    • The elevator near High Street only provides access to classrooms on all floors, not the library
    • The central elevator only provides access between floors 2 and 3 of the library
    • Accessible restrooms are located on all floors.
    • The second floor sky-bridge provides wheelchair access to the Wilson Library
  • Accessible parking on the southwest side of the building
  • See also Campus Accessibility Information
