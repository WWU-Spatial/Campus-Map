Old Main (OM)

Old Main is an Administration and Campus Services building (map) (Google directions).

ACCESSIBILITY

  • Accessible parking to the east of the building.
  • Accessible entrances on the South and East sides of the building.
  • Accessibility notes:
    • Accessible rest rooms on 1st, 3rd and 5th floors
    • South elevator gives access to floors 1-4 and only Room 530 on 5th floor
    • North elevator accesses floors 2-4 and Rooms 560-587 on 5th floor
  • See also Campus Accessibility Information
